Trip Overview

Festival Location: 4F Renaissance Festival, 132 Gosnell-Hutto Road, Danielsville, GA

Accommodation: 513 W Broad St, Athens, GA 30601

Trip Dates: September 12th - 14th

  • September 12th, 5:00pm: Depart from Lincolnton, GA toward Athens, GA.
  • Evening, September 12th: Arrive in Athens and check in at 513 W Broad St, Athens, GA 30601.
  • September 13th:
    • 10:00am – 6:00pm: Attend the 4F Renaissance Festival at 132 Gosnell-Hutto Road, Danielsville, GA.
    • Evening: Return to the accommodation, enjoy dinner, and relax.
